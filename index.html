About Us

Welcome to Jay Ganesh Kothi Ice Cream & Shrikhand, where we blend tradition with innovation to create unforgettable treats. We take pride in crafting homemade treats using locally-sourced ingredients, ensuring every scoop is infused with nostalgia and quality. Join us on a flavor-filled journey, savoring the richness of tradition and the excitement of innovation, one delightful spoonful at a time.
